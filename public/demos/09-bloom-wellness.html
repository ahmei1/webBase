Our Story

Built by women,
for women

Bloom was born from a frustration with fitness spaces that weren't designed for us. Too loud, too competitive, too focused on aesthetics over how we actually feel.

So we built something different. A studio where the goal is joy, strength, and community — not comparison or punishment.

Inclusive by design

Every body, every age, every fitness level — all welcome, always.

Women-led

Every coach, every class, every decision is led by women who get it.

Holistic wellness

Mind and body — we programme for both, every single week.
