Our Philosophy

Cuisine Born from Passion

Ember was born from a singular obsession: the transformative power of fire. Our open-flame kitchen is the heart of every dish, coaxing flavors that no other technique can achieve.

Chef Marco Alverez brings fifteen years of Michelin-starred experience to each plate — a meditation on restraint, precision, and the beauty of exceptional ingredients.

We source exclusively from local farms within 150 miles, rotating our tasting menu with the rhythms of the season.
