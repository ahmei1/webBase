Our Philosophy

Yoga is not about
perfecting the pose

Flow was founded on the belief that yoga is a practice for every body — not a performance for the flexible. We've created a space where showing up is enough, and everything else unfolds naturally.

Our instructors bring diverse training from Mysore, Bali, and beyond — united by a shared commitment to accessible, honest, and deeply nourishing practice.

"The goal of yoga is not to control your mind, but to silence it."

— B.K.S. Iyengar
What We Offer

Classes for every
journey

From the first breath to the deepest practice — we have a class to meet you exactly where you are.

01

Vinyasa Flow

Dynamic, breath-linked movement that builds strength and flexibility. Available at beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels throughout the week.

02

Yin & Restorative

Long-held poses that target connective tissue and the nervous system. The perfect antidote to a busy life — slow, quiet, and deeply effective.

03

Meditation & Breathwork

Dedicated sessions exploring pranayama, guided meditation, and mindfulness. No prior experience needed — just a willingness to sit.
